What makes the Atlantis Pro different?

🧲

Magnetic Hall Effect

No physical contact between switch components means zero wear over time. Your actuation stays consistent after 10 million keypresses — same as day one.

⚡

Rapid Trigger

Keys reset the instant you release, not at a fixed point. Faster re-press, faster movement cancel, faster peek. The competitive advantage is real.

Gasket Mount

The PCB floats on silicone gaskets, giving the board a subtle bounce that reduces finger fatigue during long sessions without sacrificing stability.
