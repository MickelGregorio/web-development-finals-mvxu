Why the Lamzu Maya X?

Weight is the most underrated variable in mouse performance. A heavier mouse requires more force to accelerate and decelerate — which means more micro-corrections, more fatigue, and less consistent aim over a long session.

The Maya X sits at 55g without sacrificing structural integrity. No honeycomb cuts. No hollow shell that flexes under a firm grip. Just a solid, rigid, lightweight ergo that tracks exactly where you move it.

Built for claw and fingertip players who've been waiting for something that actually fits their hand.
